Transform your living space with our Windows




Awning Window

Awning windows are hinged on the top and open outwards from the bottom. They provide excellent ventilation and reduce water ingress even when they are open.

Sliding Window

Sliding windows incorporate shutters which glide on rollers to the left and right. They don't take extra space when open and are ideal for hard-to-access or congested spaces.

Bay Window

They project outwards from external wall.It's a combination of three or more windows fixed at various angles. They look luxurious, create extra space and bring in more light.

Combination Window

This window allows maximum design flexibility as it can combine several window styles within a single frame. These windows address specific light and ventilation requirements.

Tilt & Turn Window

With a special hinge mechanism, these windows can open sideways like a casement window and also tilt at the top for mild ventilation.

Fixed Window

A fixed window does not open as it is fixed on all sides. It is ideal for areas which do not require ventilation but need high acoustic and thermal comfort.

Casement Window

It opens in an inward or outward direction as per the window type. It is suitable for bedroom,living room & kitchen as they can open fully to allow maximum air flow.

French Window

They are a pair of casement windows placed on the outside wall and reaching the floor.They typically open on to a garden or balcony while functioning as both windows and doors.
